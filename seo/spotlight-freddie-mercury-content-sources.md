# Content Sources & Fact-Check Verdicts — Freddie Mercury Spotlight

**File:** `content/artist-profiles/freddie-mercury.md` (draft) · **Status:** fact-checked, pending draft finalization
**Process:** per `seo/content-style-guide.md` — every substantive claim adversarially refuted through ≥2 independent
lenses (voice-science/acoustics + applied-pedagogy + biographical-record), keep SUPPORTED, hedge OVERSTATED,
fix/cut REFUTED. Never fabricate a citation; health claims reflect current clinical guidance.
**Date:** 2026-07-02

> Honest caveat up front: Freddie Mercury is unusual for a pop vocalist in that there **is** one peer-reviewed
> acoustic study of his voice (Herbst et al. 2017). That study is the anchor for the scientific claims below.
> The popular "four-octave, F2–F6" figure is **not** from that study — it comes from fan compilations and
> vocal-coach videos and is presented here (and must be in the draft) as attributed lore, hedged against the
> study's smaller *measured* range. Voice-type labels remain approximate/overlapping (range ≠ voice type).

## Open items for human validation

- **Nodules/surgery-refusal (claim 6):** no primary medical source — only Freestone-via-aggregators. Fine to keep as attributed biography; do not state as clinical fact. Confirm you're comfortable citing a fan-archive-tier source, or drop the "cancelled shows" link entirely.
- **"No vocal training" (claim 7):** genuinely UNVERIFIED. Recommend "by most accounts, no formal singing lessons" rather than an assertion. Confirm phrasing.
- **F2 / F6 extremes (claim 1):** the low F2/F#2 and high F6 come from isolated recorded moments (often backing-vocal overdubs), not sustained demonstrated singing — the study only verified up to G5. If a tighter, defensible number is wanted, lead with "F#2–G5 (measured), ~3 octaves" and treat "~4 octaves" as the popular figure.
- **Vibrato "typical" band (claim 4):** the 5.4–6.9 Hz comparison isn't a single canonical constant across the literature. Keep the point ("faster than most norms, unusually irregular") rather than over-anchoring one range.
- **Coach/embed videos:** if the draft embeds Beth Roars or other coach videos, confirm each still allows embedding before publish.
